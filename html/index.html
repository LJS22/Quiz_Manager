User Guide

Hello there! Welcome to your quiz manager. Here is a list of helpful hints to assist you with the use of this service.

Navigation

To navigate to other pages after having logged in, select the cog icon at the top right of the page. Doing so will open up a menu.

From here you will be able to access your profile page, the user guide as well as allowing you to log out.

To return to the homepage you can click on the Welcome to the Quiz Manager! title or the icon at the top left of the page.

Viewing Quizzes

Once logged in, you should see in front of you all of the quizzes that you have access to.

Click on any of the quizzes and you will be taken to a new page where you can see your quiz.

At the top left of the quiz page you will find an arrow icon, clicking on this icon will return you to the main page where you will find all of your quizzes.

Editing Quizzes

At the bottom of the quiz, you will find a button called 'Edit Mode'. Clicking on this makes every field in the quiz editable.
Any changes you wish to make, make them in the appropraite field. The larger fields contain the questions and the smaller fields the answers.
Please be aware when editing the answers, the green field indicates that that must be the correct answer. The red fields indicate it is an incorrect answer.
Once you have made all of the changes you wish, clicking on the 'Save Changes' button will update the quiz for you and turn back into a viewing mode.
If you do not wish to proceed with any of your changes, clicking the 'Cancel Edit' button will return you to viewing mode without updating the quiz.
